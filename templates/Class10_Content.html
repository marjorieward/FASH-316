Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Matching pair of desired shoulder pads
    • Desired fashion fabric(s)
    • Fusible weft interfacing
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
    • Pencil sharpener
    • Tape measure
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Fine-tip markers in various colors
    • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
    • Alphanumeric Pattern Paper
    • Needlepoint tracing wheel
    • Fashion French curve/Armsyce ruler
    • Fashion Hip Curve ruler

During Class:

  • Complete jacket/blazer pin proof and begin transferring and truing final patterns.
  • Runway Look Recreation
    • Professor will assign students with one of the selected runway looks.
    • Students will begin analysis and start fabric preparation for draping their assigned garment(s).

After Class:

  • Continue drafting final jacket patterns (including shell, facing, and lining patterns).
